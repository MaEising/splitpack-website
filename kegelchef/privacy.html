2. Summary

KegelChef is a mobile app for German bowling clubs to locally manage members, penalties, game nights, and summaries. The app works without a user account, without its own backend, without advertising, and without tracking.

  • Club and game data generally stays locally on the device.
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs are used.
  • No location, camera, microphone, or contact data is requested.
  • In-app purchases are processed through the Apple App Store.
  • Summaries are shared only if users actively trigger sharing.

3. Data processed in the app

Users can enter data into KegelChef themselves. Depending on how the app is used, this data may be personal data, especially if real names are used.

Category Examples Storage
Member data Names or nicknames, avatar selection, order, active status Locally on the device in the app database
Penalty catalog Penalty name, amount, symbol, explanation, active status Locally on the device in the app database
Game night data Session name, date, participants, penalty entries, totals Locally on the device in the app database
App settings Last visited tab, local premium status Locally on the device in app storage
Purchase status Information whether the lifetime premium package is active Locally on the device; purchase processing through Apple

Where personal data is processed, this is done under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) on the following legal bases:

Providing app functionality Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR Processing locally stored club, member, penalty, and game night data so the app can provide the requested functions.
Storing local settings Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR Storing technical and functional app state so the app remains usable.
In-app purchase and purchase restoration Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR Checking purchase status through Apple to unlock premium features.
Technical update delivery and security Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR Our legitimate interest is secure, up-to-date, and reliable app delivery. Club and game content is not transmitted to us.
Handling support and privacy requests Art. 6(1)(b), (c), and (f) GDPR Processing voluntarily submitted contact details and request contents to answer requests and comply with legal obligations.

5. Local storage on the device

KegelChef stores user-entered content locally on the device, especially in a local SQLite database and in local app storage. The app provider has no access to this locally stored club and game night data.

Data remains stored until users delete it in the app, remove app data through the operating system, or uninstall the app. Device backups may contain local app data if this is enabled in the device settings. The app provider has no access to such device or cloud backups.

6. In-app purchases through Apple

KegelChef's lifetime premium package is offered as an in-app purchase through the Apple App Store. Payment processing, invoicing, App Store accounts, and payment information are handled by Apple. KegelChef does not receive full payment details such as credit card or bank account data.

The app uses Apple-provided interfaces to check whether the premium package has been purchased or can be restored. More information about Apple's processing is available in Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Sharing summaries

KegelChef can provide a game night summary through the operating system's share function, for example for sharing in messenger apps. Transmission takes place only if users actively trigger sharing and select a target app.

The selected target app and its provider are responsible for any subsequent processing under their own privacy policies. KegelChef does not transmit these contents to the app provider.

Club, member, penalty, or game night data is not shared with the app provider or with advertising or analytics service providers.

9. Transfers to third countries

Club and game night data stored locally in KegelChef is not transferred to third countries by the app provider.

Where Apple, Expo, or the hosting provider of this privacy page process data outside the European Union or the European Economic Area, this is done under the applicable data protection mechanisms, in particular adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses.

10. Storage period

  • Local app data: until deletion in the app, reset of app data, or uninstallation of the app.
  • Local premium status: until update, reset of app data, or uninstallation of the app; the purchase can be restored through Apple.
  • Support and privacy requests: as long as necessary to process the request; beyond that only where statutory retention periods or legitimate documentation interests apply.
  • Access logs for this privacy page: 7 days.

11. No advertising, tracking, or profiling

KegelChef does not use advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or analytics services. No data is used to track users across apps or websites of other providers. There is no automated decision-making, including profiling, within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR.

12. Device permissions

Based on its current functionality, KegelChef does not request sensitive device permissions such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or calendar. Haptic feedback and the operating system share function are used only for app operation or for sharing actions actively triggered by users.

13. Privacy of this website

This HTML page uses no cookies, external fonts, external scripts, or tracking. When this page is accessed, the hosting provider processes technically necessary access data so the page can be delivered and secured. GitHub Pages is used to deliver this static page.

14. Obligation to provide data

KegelChef can be used without a user account. Users only need to enter the local data required for the desired app function. Without entering members, penalties, or game night data, the corresponding management and summary functions cannot be used, or can be used only in a limited way.

15. Rights of data subjects

Where personal data is processed and the statutory requirements are met, data subjects have the following rights in particular:

  • Access to personal data processed under Art. 15 GDPR
  • Rectification of inaccurate data under Art. 16 GDPR
  • Erasure under Art. 17 GDPR
  • Restriction of processing under Art. 18 GDPR
  • Data portability under Art. 20 GDPR
  • Objection to processing based on Art. 6(1)(e) or (f) GDPR under Art. 21 GDPR

Because club and game night data is stored locally on the device and is not available to the app provider, many rights can practically be exercised directly in the app or through the device's system settings, for example by editing or deleting local data.
